Welcome to the BIS Club at DA-IICT!

The Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) serves as the guardian of quality and safety standards in India, encompassing a wide array of sectors to ensure the well-being of our nation. In our pursuit of excellence, BIS has forged a dynamic partnership with DA-IICT, a synergy that aims to elevate standardization and conformity assessment to new heights.

At the BIS Club, we thrive on engagement and hands-on involvement. From workshops and seminars on standardization practices to collaborative research projects, we offer a diverse range of activities. Get ready to attend informative sessions, contribute to groundbreaking research, and actively participate in the creation of standards that shape the future. As a member, you'll be at the forefront of India's quality and safety initiatives.
