Are you a student at Dhirubhai Ambani Institute of Information and Communication Technology (DA-IICT) looking for an exciting opportunity to enhance your education and career prospects? Look no further! The BIS Club at DA-IICT is your gateway to a world of possibilities. Here's why you should consider joining our club:
  • Career Advancement: Involvement in standardization activities can make students more competitive in the job market. Employers often value candidates with a strong understanding of industry standards and regulations.
  • Internship opportunities: Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) offers internships to undergraduate and postgraduate students, providing hands-on experience in standardization and quality assurance. This program provides students with hands-on experience, exposure to quality standards, and networking opportunities.
  • Exposure to Real-World Challenges: Students can gain exposure to real-world challenges and issues faced by industries and organizations in adhering to standards. This practical exposure can enrich their problem-solving skills and knowledge.
  • Contribution to Industry: By participating in the DA-IICT BIS Club, students have the opportunity to contribute to the development of industry standards, which can have a significant impact on the growth and safety of emerging technologies like drones and robotics.
  • Networking Opportunities: Being part of the DA-IICT BIS Club or related activities provides students with opportunities to network with industry professionals, government officials, and experts in the field. This can lead to internships, mentorship, and potential job opportunities in the future.
  • Resume Enhancement: Participation in activities related to this collaboration can bolster students' resumes and make them more attractive candidates for graduate programs and job opportunities.
  • Professional Development: Students can develop a deeper understanding of regulatory compliance and quality standards, which are important skills in many technology-related professions.
  • Leadership and Collaboration Skills: Engaging in standardization activities can help students develop leadership and collaboration skills as they work with diverse teams and experts from various fields.
  • Hands-On Learning: Students can gain practical experience in standards development and conformity assessment processes, which are essential in industries like drones and robotics. This hands-on learning can be a valuable addition to their academic knowledge.
  • Access to Research Projects: Students may have the chance to participate in research projects related to standardization, robotics, and drones. This involvement can lead to research publications, enhancing their academic and professional profiles.
  • Knowledge Transfer: Students can transfer their knowledge and experience to other students within the DA-IICT community, creating a cycle of learning and sharing.

Ready to Make a Difference? Join Us Today!

The BIS Club at DA-IICT is more than just a club; it's a platform for you to make a real impact in the world of standards and quality. Join us, and let's work together to shape the future of technology standards in India.
