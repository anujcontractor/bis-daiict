Who we are

The BIS Club at Dhirubhai Ambani Institute of Information and Communication Technology (DA-IICT) is a dynamic and enthusiastic community dedicated to promoting and embracing the values of quality, safety, and standardization in various sectors. We are an integral part of the collaboration between DA-IICT and the Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS), which empowers us to engage actively in the standardization and conformity assessment activities that influence the technological landscape of India.

The Bureau of Indian Standards (BIS) is the National Standards Body of India, responsible for developing and promoting standards to ensure quality and safety in various sectors. BIS works in collaboration with different organization to set the product standard for Indian scenario. Recently, on 8th October BIS has signed Memorandums of Understanding (MoUs) with DA-IICT to foster collaboration in standardization and conformity assessment. The MoUs establish a framework for cooperation between BIS and DA-IICT.

Our Mission

Our mission is to foster a culture of excellence and innovation by actively participating in standardization and conformity assessment initiatives. We aim to educate, inspire, and engage our members in activities that contribute to the development of national standards and the promotion of quality and safety in emerging technologies like drones, robotics, Internet of Things (IoT), IT, agriculture etc.

What We Do

  • Participation in Standardization Activities: Our members have the unique opportunity to collaborate with BIS's technical committees at both national and international levels, actively shaping the standards that drive innovation and safety in Indian industries.
  • Infrastructure Support for R&D Projects: We support research and development by providing access to infrastructure for projects related to standardization and conformity assessment.
  • Joint Events: We organize events, seminars, and workshops that focus on standardization and conformity assessment, fostering knowledge sharing and collaboration among our members and the broader community.
  • Exchange of Publications: We facilitate the exchange of publications and information to enhance our understanding of standards and related topics, keeping our members up-to-date with the latest industry developments.
  • Center of Excellence: We aim to establish a Center of Excellence for Standardization, Testing, and Conformity Assessment, fostering further research and development in these critical areas.
  • Sharing Laboratory Facilities: Our collaboration may include sharing laboratory facilities, enabling our members to conduct testing and assessment activities in line with BIS standards.

Why Join Us

Joining the BIS Club at DA-IICT offers an exceptional opportunity to enhance your knowledge, network with industry professionals, and contribute to the development of national standards. Whether you're a student with a passion for emerging technologies or an academic eager to make a difference, our club provides the platform to grow, learn, and lead in the field of standardization.
